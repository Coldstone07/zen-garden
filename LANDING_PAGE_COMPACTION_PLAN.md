- Ages 18â€“40, higher online activity (social media, gaming, shopping)

**Pain Points**:

- Use **belonging language** ("Join 200+ leaders")

- Provide **boundary-setting tools** early
- Frame as **digital health**, not moralization

**Landing Page Frame**:
> "Break the validation cycle. Find real presence."

- Ages 20â€“60, higher depression/anxiety (clinically diagnosed or suspected)

**Pain Points**:
1. Trauma symptoms (flashbacks, hypervigilance, dissociation)

3. Anxiety disorders preventing work/relationships

**Motivators**:

- **Research backing** ("Is this evidence-based?")
- **Gentleness** ("Not another 'optimize yourself' program")

**Content Strategy**:
- Lead with **science & credentials**
- Use **trauma-informed language** (no shame, no blame)
- Highlight **professional training** (trauma-informed coaching, somatic)
- Offer **low-barrier entry** (discovery call, not immediate commitment)

**Landing Page Frame**:
> "Healing grounded in science. Delivered with compassion."
