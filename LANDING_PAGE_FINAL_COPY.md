# Kairos Landing Page: Final Copy Strategy

**Status**: Ready to hand to designer/developer
**Tone**: Vulnerable, honest, human-centered (see EMAIL_TO_TEAM.md for reference)
**Purpose**: Every word shows WHY we're saying it

## PART 1: CORE MESSAGE & TONE GUIDELINES

### The One Thing We're Saying
"You've achieved everything you were supposed to achieve. But external success doesn't guarantee internal peace. And that's not a failure—that's just being human. Here's what becomes possible when you stop running and start living."

### Tone Rules
- ✅ Vulnerable (we've walked this path)
- ✅ Recognition (you're not broken)
- ✅ Hope (change is real and possible)
- ✅ Community (you're not alone)
- ❌ Jargon (no "psycho-spiritual," "evidence-informed," "integral frameworks")
- ❌ Credentials-first (stories before credentials)
- ❌ Quick fixes ("12-week transformation," "optimize your life")

### Reference Tone Examples From EMAIL_TO_TEAM.md
- "So we stepped back and asked a different question: What does the world actually need from Kairos?"
- "The original plan was solid—data-driven, outcomes-focused, built on methodology and metrics. But it didn't feel *true*..."
- "This is AA-model positioning. Community-first, not performance-first."

## PART 2: 10-SECTION LANDING PAGE COPY

### SECTION 1: RECOGNITION (Hero Equivalent)

**Purpose**: Make visitors feel SEEN within 5 seconds. No pitch. Just recognition.

**WHY THIS FIRST**: Burnt-out achievers don't want to hear about frameworks or outcomes. They want to know: "Am I the only one who feels this way?" The answer is NO. And that matters more than any methodology.

**COPY**:
```
You've Achieved Everything You Were Supposed To.

So why does it feel so empty?

You've hit the goals. Built the career. Made the money.
Checked the boxes.

But inside?

You're running on fumes.

Connected to everyone and everything except yourself.
Optimizing everything except your own life.
Succeeding at everything except being present.

You're not broken.
You're not lazy.
You're not ungrateful.

You're human.

And you're not alone.

---

What if stopping the running was the beginning?

What if you could have success *and* presence?
Achievement *and* peace?
Ambition *and* aliveness?

That's what becomes possible here.

Not in 12 weeks. In real time. With real people.
Through real work.

Ready?
```

**CTA**: "Let's Talk About This" (single, conversational button)

**Design Notes**:
- Dark, contemplative background (not flashy)
- White/cream text, readable instantly
- No animations or distractions
- Mobile-first (copy stacks cleanly)

**Content Status**: ✅ Ready (already live on website)

### SECTION 7: THE THREE PHASES

**Purpose**: Show structure simply. Not detailed methodology. Clear roadmap.

**WHY HERE**: After guides, show what the journey looks like. Keep it simple—3 phases. No jargon.

**COPY**:
```
How This Works: Three Phases

PHASE 1: DISCOVER
Understand what's really happening.
Why you're running.
What you actually need (not what you think you need).
Who you actually are (underneath the achievement).

Duration: 4-6 weeks
What happens: Weekly conversations. Journaling. Community calls.
What shifts: Recognition. "Oh. That's what's happening."

---

PHASE 2: INTEGRATE
Weave this understanding into your actual life.
Sleep better. Show up differently with people you love.
Make choices that align with who you actually are.
Stop running. Start choosing.

Duration: 6-8 weeks
What happens: Guided practice. Community accountability. Ongoing support.
What shifts: Behavior. Presence. Relationships. Sleep.

---

PHASE 3: ACTUALIZE
Live what you've learned.
Lead from wholeness, not performance.
Be ambitious AND alive.
Help others find their way back (many do).

Duration: Ongoing
What happens: Mastery. Leadership from your actual values.
What shifts: Everything. Your definition of success changes.

---

Not a timeline. A progression.
Not a program. A journey.
Not alone. In community.
```

**Design Notes**:
- 3 columns (desktop), stacked (mobile)
- Visual progression (arrows connecting phases)
- Each phase: title + 3-4 line description max
- Icons optional (discover = compass, integrate = roots, actualize = wings)
