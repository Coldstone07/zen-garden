## ‚ùì COMMON QUESTIONS

**"Is this too risky? Are we giving up on credibility?"**
No. Vulnerability actually builds MORE credibility than credentials. People trust people who've walked the path. Not people who've studied the path.

**"Will burnt-out achievers respond to this?"**
Yes. Better than the old approach. Because this SEES them. It doesn't try to sell them. It invites them into community.

**"What if people want the methodology explained?"**
They can have it. But not on page 1. On page 10. Or on a secondary page. We lead with recognition, not education.

**"Are we abandoning the original Compaction Plan?"**
The structure is still useful (10 sections, clear CTAs, etc.). But the *content* completely changes. We're keeping the bones, replacing the heart.

**"How do we know this will work?"**
We don't. That's why we're testing it. First with the core landing page (Weeks 2-4). Then we measure (Week 4 onward): Do people feel SEEN? Do they want to have a conversation? Do they join?
